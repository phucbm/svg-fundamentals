## d attribute

- https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/SVG/Attribute/d
- `l`: line to (relative coordinate)
- `L`: line to (absolute coordinate)
- `h` and `H`: horizontal line
- `v` and `V`: vertical line
